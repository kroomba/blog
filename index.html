april 27 2023

Today was amazing. I went to the [april] awards not expecting to win anything, and instead, we managed to win in every category we participated in. I even won people’s choice. It was so fun.

However, after the event she approached me...

We hadn’t talked in a while ever since, you know.

But as I was walking away, she called after me. Someone nearby told me, “Hey, I think some girl is calling you.”

She ran up to me and spread her arms, gesturing in amazement.

It was weird. She was proud of me. Like I was her friend. Up until this point, I was nervous to consider ourselves as such.

It’s kind of weird. She had worked her whole life for this. Her entire thing was “film.” She wanted to be a director. She wanted to make movies. She wanted to go to film school. She was the broadcast girl.

And yet...

Here I was, with no prior experience, no prior ambition, nothing. Just a random person who thought it would be cool to try and enter into this film competition.

And I won. And she didn’t.

I was now the film guy. She was the broadcast girl, and nothing more.

Part of me felt bad. But she seemed so excited for me.

She kept mentioning teaming up, doing films together. Later, she pressured me to join the video production team at the school, to help make the BHS broadcast.

I don't really know what to make of this. I don't know what we are. I really don't know who she is anymore. She sounded like she...

I don't know where this came from, but I can't get her voice out of my head.

Her soft voice,

Saying over and over

Help help help help help help help help
